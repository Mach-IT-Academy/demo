The Mach IT Academy: Tech+ Edition Interactive Training Guide is a fully browser-based, self-paced training experience built around a single philosophy: understand the concept, then prove it. Every module moves you from instruction to application — because recognition is not the same as competency, and the CompTIA Tech+ exam knows the difference.

This guide was built by a certified IT professional and educator who has personally sat for and passed the CompTIA Tech+ exam and the CompTIA A+ exam. Every objective, every scenario, and every question reflects what you will actually encounter — not what textbooks assume you will encounter.

// Study Guide Structure

Five Content Types. One Proven Path.

Each module follows a deliberate learning progression through five activity types:

??
STEP 01  —  INSTRUCTION

INSTR — Interactive Blended Instruction

Instructional lessons that teach the core concept and include an embedded interactive activity to reinforce it before you move on. This is where learning begins.

??
STEP 02  —  REINFORCEMENT

REIN PBQ

Performance-based questions that expand on the concepts introduced in the Instr. These bridge instruction and application by challenging you to recall and apply what you just learned.

??
STEP 03  —  APPLICATION

EXER PBQ

Scenario-based exercises that connect real-world workplace situations to the underlying IT concepts. You are not just answering a question — you are solving a problem in context.

???
STEP 04  —  HANDS-ON

Lab PBQ

Self-contained, browser-based simulations for setting up devices, configuring systems, and completing technical tasks — entirely in the browser. No external tools required.

??
STEP 05  —  EXAM READINESS

SCEN QUIZ

Exam-style quizzes for each CompTIA objective modeled after the CompTIA Tech+ exam. These test your readiness across a mix of concept knowledge, applied reasoning, and scenario interpretation.
